Privacy Policy

App: LudiParc Meknès • Last updated: October 7, 2025

We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how LudiParc Meknès handles user data and the limited circumstances in which data may be collected or used.

1) Data we collect

The app collects only the following data when you choose to use the contact option:

  • Email address — collected if you voluntarily send us a message through the in-app contact option.

No other personal or sensitive data (such as location, contacts, camera, microphone, or storage) is collected.

2) Purpose of use

  • App functionality — enables the contact feature to work.
  • Developer communications — allows us to reply to your inquiries and support requests.

3) Processing & retention

  • Ephemeral processing: your email address is processed only at the moment you send your message.
  • The app does not store personal data locally or on our servers.
  • Messages are sent via your device’s email app and may be retained in your outbox and our inbox according to normal email practices.

4) Data sharing

We do not share your data with third parties. Collected data is used only to respond to your inquiry.

5) Security

Messages are transmitted securely by your email provider where supported. The app itself does not transmit data over insecure channels.

6) Data deletion

You can request deletion of any email/messages you have sent us at any time:

  • Send an email to contact@ludiparc.live with the email address you used, or
  • Uninstall the app to remove any local data stored on your device.

7) Communication features

  • The app provides buttons that open your device’s default apps (Email, Phone, or WhatsApp) to contact us.
  • We do not access your messages or call logs; external apps are governed by their own privacy policies.

8) Children’s privacy

The app is not directed to children, and it does not collect personal information from children.

9) Changes to this policy

We may update this policy to reflect app changes. Updates will be posted here with a new “Last updated” date.
